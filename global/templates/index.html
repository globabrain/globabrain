Aims and Objectives

(A) We encourage , promote and conduct collaborative research in partnership with universities, colleges and polytechnics all over the world. We support research by providing platforms on which intellectual discussions can be made The society is working on establishing a center for innovation and excellence. To this effect, properties have been acquired to build laboratories. The goal is for students to be able to run experiments for research purposes at extremely low costs. This will facilitate the development of Science and Technology for intelligent but needy students. Experts in specific fields shall be invited to teach/give teleconferences in these areas. For example, the use of open-path Fourier Transform Infrared Spectrometry to detect chemical warfare agents involves combined knowledge in vibrational spectroscopy and chemometrics. Specialized areas like these that will be great for our law enforcement agencies including the military, police service, fire service, etc will involve inviting experts from around the world to deliver lectures.

(B) Academic and Business Conferences Conferences are organized yearly . These will be formal events where researchers present results, showcase workshops, and other activities and also businesses discussing technical and/or business-related matters. This provides an excellent platform for the reflection of academic research to industrial success. This exposition gives you the opportunity to get a hands-on look at the latest instrumentation, participate in live demonstrations and product seminars, talk with technical experts, and find solutions to all your research challenges. Abstracts are submitted five months to the event. Research work is presented as poster and oral presentations.

(C) Awards: Outstanding researchers and industries, including students are recognized with awards. Awards are also given to minister of states, heads of states, academic institutions, and individuals for excellent policies towards scientific innovations and development and enhancement/betterment of the lives of citizenry.

(D) The society networks institutions with limited funds to big sources of information internationally to enhance research and development

(E) Our Journals and Editorial Board endeavor to attract and publish articles, research thesis and dissertations from a variety of disciplines and educational settings with the goal of promoting excellence. We publish news on business, scientific and technological innovations. Individuals, governments, non-governmental organizations, and industries are encouraged to publish and/or advertise outcomes and successes. Authors will be contacted to submit their payments and to complete copyright forms to have their manuscripts published. We will contact you to retract your paper if we notice that the work has been published in another journal at an earlier date

(F) We support by providing patent lawyers for filing invention records. This protects your property right as lots of innovation from the developing world are often not properly protected, leading to loss to the developed world.

(G) We provide a platform for advertising jobs. This gives the employers the best opportunity to hire the best employees and allows employees to decide on who to work for. We have experts who can provide technical advice on resume writing.
